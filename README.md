# Sales-insights-Dashboard 

This project is an interactive Sales Insights Dashboard built using SQL and Power BI to analyze sales performance and provide actionable business insights.

Features
Revenue and Sales Quantity KPI cards
Revenue trend analysis over time
Revenue by market
Sales quantity by market
Top 5 customers by revenue
Top 5 products by revenue
Interactive Year and Date slicers
Dynamic filtering and drill-down analysis
Tools Used
SQL – Data extraction and transformation
Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
DAX – Measures and calculated metrics
Power Query – Data cleaning and preparation
Business Insights
Identified top-performing markets based on revenue.
Analyzed monthly revenue trends to identify seasonal patterns.
Evaluated customer and product performance.
Enabled interactive exploration of sales data through filters and slicers.
